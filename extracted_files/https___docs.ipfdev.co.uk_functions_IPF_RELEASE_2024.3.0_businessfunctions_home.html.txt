Business Functions
Business Functions are a set of out of the box supporting components for your IPF payments solution. They take the form of reusable components that you can use within your IPF process flows.
Business Functions are supported by all the necessary underlying java implementations and dependencies needed to fully implement the function.
It is a simple process to make Business Functions available for use within your IPF solution.
